About

I am a junior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science and Technology at Peking University. My current research interests lie in computer vision and generative AI, with a focus on image synthesis, all-in-one image restoration, and multimodal evaluation.

I am a research intern at the Camera Intelligence Lab, advised by Prof. Boxin Shi, and previously worked with Prof. Tingting Jiang at VIE Group on visual benchmark construction for multimodal evaluation.
